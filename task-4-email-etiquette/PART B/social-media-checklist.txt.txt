Do’s

Be thoughtful before sharing anything online and consider its impact on yourself and others.
Use strong passwords and enable privacy settings for your accounts.
Always verify the facts before sharing to avoid disseminating false information.
Practice respectful and polite behavior online.
Use social media constructively for your learning, networking, and collaborative activities.



Don’ts

Don’t share your personal or sensitive information online.
Don’t practice cyberbullying, trolling, or hate speech online.
Don’t share anything online that may impact your academic or professional reputation.
Don’t spread false news or rumors by believing them or sharing them without verifying them.
Don’t let social media take over your life by compromising your studies and responsibilities.
