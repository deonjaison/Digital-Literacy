Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students in India

Use unique, strong passwords for all accounts. Enable 2-factor Authentication (2FA) where ever possible.
Never share OTP, PIN, CVV, UPI approval requests, even if it’s from a bank/customer care. (Financial/UPI Safety)
Do not scan any unknown QR codes, nor click on any suspicious links received through SMS, Email, WhatsApp, etc. (Financial/UPI Safety)
Always keep your phones, laptops, apps, antivirus, etc., updated to avoid any security issues.
Always keep privacy settings high for all your social media accounts. Don’t share your phone number, address, college ID, etc.
Always be very cautious when using public WiFi. Avoid logging into any banking apps, etc.
Always verify the online seller, job offers, internships, giveaways, etc., before making any transactions.
Always log out of any accounts after use, especially when using public computers. Keep your device locked with a PIN/biometric security.


What to do if you are a victim of cybercrime?
Report on the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
Contact the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
